Elevate

A financial game that trains a player's predictive capability on deciding whether to buy or sell a stock.

How the game works:

  • You will be given a information about a stock on a random date.
  • The information includes the closing price, change in price since opening, sentiment of news articles on the stock published that day, a few technical analysis indicators, and a graph of the stock's price for a time interval.
  • Your goal is to decide whether to buy or sell the stock.
  • You win if you either buy and the price goes up or sell and the price goes down. Note the price compared is the closing price of the stock on the following day.
